Key Projects

Public Health Neuquén: Andes

ANDES is a digital platform designed for patients and health teams, enabling secure and efficient access and management of health data. It serves as a technological interface for HUDS (Unified Digital Health Record), a collection of digitized health records from public and private healthcare providers. The platform follows international standards for structuring health information.

Design Interactive: Xmentor

Xmentor is an Extended Reality (XR) training platform developed by Design Interactive, designed to deliver immersive, scalable, and cost-effective training solutions for health and industrial environments. It leverages Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) to enhance learning, provide real-time procedural guidance, facilitate remote collaboration, and improve overall training outcomes on mobile and web platforms.

Blackthorn: Payments and Events

Blackthorn is a comprehensive payment and event management platform built to optimize billing, invoicing, event registration, and fundraising processes. It provides scalable and secure solutions for organizations to seamlessly manage their financial and event operations, offering deep integration with Salesforce and other platforms. The platform enables real-time payment processing, subscription management, and event coordination to enhance user experience and operational efficiency.
