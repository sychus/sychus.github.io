About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer with over 18 years of experience in software development, specialized in Health Informatics for the last 11 years. My passion for technology goes beyond code: I love designing scalable architectures, leading teams, and creating solutions that truly impact people's lives.

Throughout my career, I have worked on critical healthcare sector projects, from hospital systems to public health platforms. I have led multidisciplinary teams, taught university classes, and contributed to open source projects. My approach has always been to combine Clean Code, agile methodologies and industry best practices.

Currently, I work as a Tech Lead at Blackthorn, where I develop modern web applications using Angular, Node.js, and both NoSQL and relational databases. I consider myself a curious developer, always learning new technologies and sharing knowledge with the community.
