Key Projects

Public Health Neuquén: Andes

ANDES is a digital platform designed for patients and healthcare teams, enabling secure and efficient access and management of health data. It serves as the technological interface for the HUDS (Unified Digital Health History), a collection of digitized health records from both public and private healthcare providers. The platform follows international standards to structure health information.

Design Interactive: Xmentor

Xmentor is an Extended Reality (XR) training platform developed by Design Interactive, designed to deliver immersive, scalable, and cost-effective training solutions for healthcare and industrial environments. It leverages Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) to enhance learning, provide real-time procedure guidance, facilitate remote collaboration, and improve overall training outcomes across mobile and web platforms.

Blackthorn: Payments and Events

Blackthorn is a comprehensive payments and events management platform built to streamline billing, invoicing, event registration, and fundraising processes. It provides scalable and secure solutions for organizations to manage their financial and event operations seamlessly, offering deep integration with Salesforce and other platforms. The platform enables real-time payment processing, subscription management, and event coordination to enhance the user experience and operational efficiency.
